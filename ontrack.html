Ontrack Focus

Ontrack Focus - A useful app used to keep you focused.

It is simple to use, and can easily run in the backround without lagging your computer.

All you have to do is enter your task and a time, then leave the app alone.

Once you have spent sufficient time performing your task, Focus will let you know by playing a sound.

Note that this is still in development, so features and gui may not be the same as it appears in screenshots
